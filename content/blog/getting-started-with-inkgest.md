## Your first draft in three steps

1. **Add a source** — Paste a URL or bring in the page you want to turn into a newsletter or article.
2. **Describe the angle** — One short sentence is enough: tone, audience, and what you want readers to do next.
3. **Edit and ship** — Treat the draft as a starting point: tighten the hook, adjust sections, and lock in your CTA.

Inkgest is built for iteration. The goal is not a perfect first pass; it is a structured draft you can refine in minutes instead of hours.

## What to look for in the editor

- **Clear sections** — Headings and paragraphs you can reorder without rewriting everything.
- **A visible CTA** — Every piece should end with one obvious next step for the reader.
- **Room for your voice** — Swap phrases, add examples, and remove anything that does not sound like you.

When you are ready, export or copy into your email tool and send. If you want to go deeper, explore [Inkgest](https://inkgest.com) and run another pass with a sharper angle.
